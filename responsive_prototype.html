PROJECT MISSION

As a BrandX project, our mission was to deliver a new, clean and updated, responsive design for the travel platform clickandgo.com Throughout our deliverables, we went over the creation of a new look and feel, encouraging our client to clarify information hierarchy and deciding for a clearer user journey.

More about the project

PLACING THE SEARCH IN THE CENTER OF THE USER JOURNEY

My first contribution was to create a layout system that gave the search process a central place. Instead of isolating it in on a side panel as was previously the case, the search panel received an exclusive, central positioning in order to meet with user's core task: find the desired holiday.

AHH, HOLIDAYS! IT'S ABOUT DREAMING AND DISCOVERY! 

Finding the good travel offers is important, but deciding for the right one is a matter of emotions. Holidays it's all about emotions. In redesigning the platform, I made place to full-screen images to showcase featured opportunities. Moreover, minimalistic and flat components were meant to let locations' images to stand out and favorite discovery.

DESIGNING THE BLOG

If the overall platform was already structured, the Blog needed a special attention. I started by doing wireframes, trying and testing multiple layouts. The search panel was moved to a left side panel in order to let the posts' content come up, while keeping the ability to find and book a trip on the user's loop.

A FULLY RESPONSIVE GRID SYSTEM

The prototype was designed within a modular, column-based layout system: starting with a 320px width for a holiday offer thumbnail, we created three layout type with breaking points to fit large, medium and mobile screens. The large ones could accommodate 4 thumbnail columns, the medium 3 and finally one column for smartphones.

TRY THE PROTOTYPE SEE WEBSITE

With a new, clean and responsive prototype for their traveling business, ClickandGo had a good instrument to start renewing their platform. This meant not only a look and feel refresh, but a thoughtful process that had put the core user journey at the center of the structure. While implementing the redesign ClickandGo saw their booking grow and we were happy to have given our contribution.
